Agreement Terms

1. Engagement & Services

The Company engages the Instructor to provide taekwondo instruction, training, and related educational services on behalf of JUNBI Pro. The specific scope, schedule, and location of Services shall be mutually agreed upon in writing or via the Company's scheduling platform.

2. Independent Contractor Status

The Instructor is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, agent, or joint venturer of the Company. The Instructor shall not be entitled to any employee benefits. The Instructor is solely responsible for all applicable taxes, including self-employment taxes.

3. Compensation

The Company shall compensate the Instructor according to the following package structure:

a) Starter Package — $500: Includes nine (9) training drills plus one (1) intro/promo video. Payment is made upon delivery of the completed content.

b) Continuation Packages — $200 each: Each continuation package includes five (5) additional training drills. Once the Instructor reaches fifteen (15) drills live on the platform, continuation packages increase to $300 each. Continuation packages are offered at the sole discretion of the Company. The Instructor has no obligation or entitlement beyond the initial Starter Package, and the Company is under no obligation to offer additional packages.

There is no cap on continuation packages should the Company choose to offer them. For reference:

• Starter only (9 drills + 1 intro video): $500
• Starter + 2 continuations (19 drills): $900
• Starter + 5 continuations (34 drills): $1,500
• Starter + 10 continuations (59 drills): $2,500

c) Payment Terms: The Company shall issue payment within thirty (30) days of approved submitted videos. The Instructor must submit completed video content for review and approval prior to payment being processed.

d) Content Revisions: The Company reserves the right to request revisions to submitted video content prior to approval. The Instructor shall complete up to two (2) rounds of revisions per video at no additional cost. Revisions beyond two rounds shall be subject to mutual agreement between the Parties.

4. Instructor Conduct

The Instructor agrees to uphold the standards and reputation of JUNBI Pro at all times. In particular:

a) The Instructor shall not reference, mention, or display the name "ATA" (American Taekwondo Association) or any ATA-affiliated branding, logos, or materials in any instructional videos, digital content, or promotional materials produced in connection with JUNBI Pro.

b) JUNBI Pro does not facilitate private lessons. The Instructor shall not accept, arrange, or conduct any private lessons with students without first obtaining written permission from the student's primary instructor of record.

c) All instructional videos must be filmed while wearing the official JUNBI shirt provided by the Company at no cost to the Instructor.

5. Video Content Exclusivity

JUNBI Pro shall retain exclusive rights to all instructional video content produced by the Instructor. Videos shall be hosted exclusively on junbitraining.com and shall not be published on any other platform without prior written permission of the Company. For the avoidance of doubt, all video content shall remain published on the JUNBI Pro platform following termination of this Agreement, and the Instructor shall have no right to request removal of such content.

6. Confidentiality

The Instructor agrees to keep confidential all proprietary information, business strategies, curricula, student data, and training methodologies for the term of this Agreement and for two (2) years following termination.

7. Intellectual Property

Any instructional materials, video content, curricula, or training programs created in connection with the Services shall be works made for hire and the exclusive property of the Company.

8. Likeness & Promotional Rights

The Instructor grants the Company a perpetual, royalty-free, worldwide license to use the Instructor's name, likeness, image, voice, biography, and photographs in connection with the promotion, marketing, and distribution of JUNBI Pro content, including use on junbitraining.com, social media channels, advertising materials, and third-party promotional partnerships. This license shall survive termination of this Agreement.

9. Liability & Indemnification

The Instructor shall maintain appropriate personal liability insurance and agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company from any claims arising from the Instructor's performance of Services, negligence, or breach of this Agreement.

10. Term & Termination

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date. The Instructor's initial commitment is limited to the Starter Package described in Section 3(a). Beyond the Starter Package, no ongoing term or obligation exists unless the Company offers, and the Instructor accepts, additional Continuation Packages. Either Party may terminate with thirty (30) days' written notice. The Company may terminate immediately for cause. Sections 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, and 9 shall survive termination.

11. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with applicable state laws. Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration.

12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior understandings. It may only be amended in writing signed by both Parties.
